Praxis
training data infrastructure for robotics
mission

We are building the largest provider of high-quality, high-volume training data for large-scale robotic models — treating data production as industrial infrastructure, not a research problem.

approach

Every major technological shift has been a data shift. Robotics is next — and the scale of the data problem dwarfs anything that came before it.

We study what makes robot training data actually work. Then we produce it at a cost and scale no one else can match. We collect the data that actually matters. The kind that's hard to get — in real environments, under real conditions, with operators who know what they're doing. Every capture is clean. Every edge case is documented. Every dataset ships verified, versioned, and ready to train on.
