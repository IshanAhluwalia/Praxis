The Bottleneck: Real-World Experience

Progress in embodied AI is bottlenecked by rich, real-world experience, not model architectures.

Current Data Requirements

  • Task-conditioned (explicit goals, instructions, rewards)
  • Multimodal (vision, force/torque, proprioception, language, audio)

Existing Insufficient Solutions

High control but low realism, small environments, low throughput

Opportunistic Collection

Solution: Data as an Industrial Process

Praxis treats data production as manufacturing, not research.

Standardized Protocols

Task-specific protocols codified as SOPs with standardized capture rigs and trained human operators.

Industrial Control

Rigorous QA, validation, and dataset versioning optimized for consistency and controlled realism.

Failure-Inclusive Data

Explicitly captures errors, recoveries, and edge cases since deployment robustness depends on off-nominal behavior.

Real-World Environments

Purpose-built test environments and live operational settings for warehousing, retail, and light manufacturing.
