Praxis
Industrial data for embodied AI.
// mission

We manufacture the physical-world experience that robots need to learn — treating data production as infrastructure, not research.

// approach
  • Purpose-built environments across industrial, domestic, and urban settings
  • Standardized capture rigs — vision, force/torque, proprioception, language
  • Human operators executing tasks, interventions, and failure recovery
  • End-to-end QA, dataset versioning, and pipeline delivery
// advantage

Based in India. ~200 acres of deployable land. ~1,000 trained operators. Structurally difficult to replicate elsewhere.
