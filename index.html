Praxis

We are building the world's largest provider of high-quality, large-scale training data for robotic foundation models. Our goal is to turn data production into industrial infrastructure.

approach

Data is the resource modern intelligence is built on. Robotics is entering that phase now, and the scale of the data challenge is far greater than anything AI has faced before.

We focus on the data that actually matters. Real interactions in real environments, captured under real operating conditions. Our datasets are collected by trained operators and designed to capture difficult edge cases that rarely appear in controlled lab settings.

Every dataset is clean, structured, and verified.

Every capture is documented and reproducible.

Every release is versioned and ready for training.
