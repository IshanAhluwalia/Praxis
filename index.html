Praxis

We are building the world's largest provider of training data for robotic foundation models, turning data production into permanent industrial infrastructure.

the shift

Every major technological transition has been preceded by a data transition. Robotics is entering that phase now. The challenge is orders of magnitude harder than anything the software or LLM era required. Embodied systems need real physical interaction, not scraped text or simulated environments.

what we do

We collect what actually matters: high-fidelity interactions in real environments, under real operating conditions, at scale. Trained operators. Documented edge cases. Every dataset ships clean, verified, versioned, and ready to train on.
